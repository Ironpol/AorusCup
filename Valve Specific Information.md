## Seeding & Additional Information

### Seeding

For seeding purposes, the Aorus Cup - Edition 2 will use the official Global VRS Ranking published by Valve in March 2026.

Seeding will be applied exclusively to the Playoffs stage (Top 8 teams).

If a team does not appear in the VRS ranking, they will be seeded below all ranked teams and placed at the discretion of the Tournament Administration.

---

### Additional Information

• No VRS filter and no VRS invitations. Sign-ups are open to all eligible teams through the national qualifiers.

• There are no direct invites. All teams must qualify through their respective national qualifier (UK, Sweden, Denmark, Spain, Germany, Poland, Turkey, France).

• The event is open to teams from the Global Region, in accordance with Valve's official rules & regulations. However, each national qualifier requires a minimum core of 3 players from the respective country.

• The entire tournament is played online via FACEIT, including both the National Qualifiers and the Playoffs.

• There are no additional compensations for teams or players outside of the €10,000 prize pool.

• The event is structured to align with Valve's Ranked Tier 2 competitive standards. However, no direct VRS invitations will be issued.

---

### Tournament Dates

National Qualifiers: 12th March - 20th March 2026  
Playoffs (Top 8): 21st March - 22nd March 2026  

All matches are played online.
